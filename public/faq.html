FAQ

1.What is the Million Dollar Insta Page?
The Million Dollar Insta Page is a digital billboard where each pixel costs $1. You can buy a block of pixels, upload your image or logo, and link it to your own website or profile — owning a permanent spot in internet history.

2.How many pixels are there in total?
There are exactly 2,000,000 pixels available on the homepage (arranged in a 2000x2000 grid).

3.How much does it cost to buy pixels?
Each pixel costs $1. You can buy a minimum of 100 pixels to ensure your image is visible.

4.What can I use my pixels for?
You can upload an image (logo, meme, photo, etc.) and link it to your website, blog, product, social profile, or anything you want.

5.Is my purchase permanent?
Yes, once you purchase your pixels and your image is approved, your spot is permanently displayed on the site.

6. Can I change my image or link later?
To maintain integrity, changes are not allowed after purchase, so make sure everything is correct before submitting.

7.Is this a scam? Why should I trust this?
Absolutely not. This project is inspired by the original 2005 Million Dollar Homepage. Every transaction is transparent and each buyer gets a confirmed, public spot.

8.Do I get a receipt or proof of purchase?
Yes, after your payment is confirmed, you’ll receive an email receipt and a unique confirmation ID for your pixel block.

9.What image formats are accepted?
We support PNG, JPG, and GIF formats. The image should be clear even at small sizes.

10. What happens when all pixels are sold?
Once all pixels are sold, the site becomes a locked digital art piece, preserved online forever as a unique record of participation.
